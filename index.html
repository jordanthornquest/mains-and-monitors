So far out of the box, but not so far out of my mind.

Listen to my latest single

Jordan Thornquest is a California-born, Idaho-raised musician who is in love with lyrical storytelling, driving chords, suitcase kick-drums, and croony blues. Through his music, Thornquest takes aim at personal events, minor tragedies, sad friends, going to college (and why you don't have to) and why corgis are the best, by singing about them with a snarky grin, firing off with a genre blending concoction of alternative indie-rock, gritty folk, blazing punk, and blues-and-soul-influenced licks & melodies.

For inquiries and booking, contact me via email, Facebook, or Twitter.
