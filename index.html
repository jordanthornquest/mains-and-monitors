Jordan Thornquest is a California-born, Idaho-raised musician who is in love with lyrical storytelling, driving chords, noodly guitar solos, and earwormy hooks. Through his music, Thornquest takes aim at personal events, sad friends, going to college (and why you don't have to), and why corgis are the best. Singing & striding with a snarky grin, Thornquest fires off with a genre-blending concoction of alternative indie-rock, blazing punk, and blues-and-soul-influenced licks & melodies.

For inquiries and booking, contact me via email, Facebook, or Twitter.
